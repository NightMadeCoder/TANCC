Fusing Understanding and Data to Foster Community Capacity and Climate Resilience

TANCC (The African Narrative on Climate Change) is driving actionable dialogue around climate change in Africa, by supporting community resilience initiatives and amplifying the voices leading the charge towards a sustainable future.

Diving deep into African innovation and resilience, our podcast series paints a human picture behind the statistics.

OUR APPROACH

We Foster Advocacy And Facilitate Discussions That Ensure Every Voice Is Heard , Bringing Together A Diverse Range Of Stakeholders.

Our Vision

To craft a future where Africa stands resilient, informed, and proactive in the face of global climate challenges while celebrating its unique contributions, innovations, and solutions in the environmental sphere.
